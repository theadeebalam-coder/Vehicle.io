What is Bharat.io?

Bharat.io is designed for modern cities where privacy, safety and quick communication matter. Instead of writing phone numbers on vehicles, owners place a unique QR sticker that anyone can scan to reach them during genuine parking or emergency situations.

Vehicle owners voluntarily register their contact details once. After registration, the QR works automatically — no apps, no logins, no tracking, and no misuse for marketing or spam.

How It Works

1

Register Once

The vehicle owner registers their vehicle number, mobile number, and optional business profile details on Bharat.io.

2

Paste QR Sticker

A unique QR sticker is placed on the vehicle. No personal information is visible on the vehicle itself.

3

Scan & Connect

Anyone facing a parking issue or emergency scans the QR and connects with the vehicle owner instantly through the website.

Why Bharat.io?

Privacy First

Your phone number is never printed on the vehicle. It is shared only when someone scans the QR for a genuine reason.

Emergency Use Only

The system is strictly meant for parking assistance and emergency communication. Promotional or spam usage is discouraged.

Business Visibility

Vehicle owners can optionally add their profession or business description, helping them gain visibility without exposing privacy.
